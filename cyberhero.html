CyberHERo Community

CyberHERo is dedicated to empowering women in cybersecurity by fostering diversity, mentorship, and innovation. Our mission is to inspire change, build a more inclusive community, and shape the future of the cybersecurity industry. CyberHERo welcomes everyone, women, allies, supporters, and champions, who share a commitment to breaking down barriers and creating opportunities for women to thrive at every stage of their careers. Through education, collaboration, and advocacy, we aim to strengthen professional networks, foster mentorship, and ensure the next generation of cybersecurity leaders is as diverse as the challenges they will face.

Founded and ran by Elisha!
