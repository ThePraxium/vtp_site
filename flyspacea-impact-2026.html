Section 07 — Respect, by Design

Some Things We Will Never Show.

OPSEC — No CENTCOM Flights
"Unlike others, we don't display CENTCOM flights. Out of respect for our service members, that region stays off every map and feed we publish. If you see CENTCOM Space-A flights posted anywhere, it's wrong."
Privacy — The Minimum Data, Nothing More

A commitment to minimal data retention and standing compliance. Deletion requests are honored consistently.

Discipline — No Tail Numbers. Ever

Tail numbers and aircraft identifiers are never stored or displayed, preventing database leaks.
